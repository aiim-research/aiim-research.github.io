Introducing Our New AIIM Research Group Website!

October 25, 2023

Dear AIIM Research Group Members and Enthusiasts,

We are thrilled to announce the launch of our brand-new website for the AIIM (Artificial Intelligence and Information Mining) Research Group. This website is a significant milestone in our journey, and it will serve as a hub for all things related to AI, Data Mining, and Machine Learning within our community.

Key Features of Our New Website:

  • Latest Research: Stay updated with the cutting-edge research and projects undertaken by our members.
  • Resources: Access a wealth of resources, including publications, datasets, and educational materials.
  • Events: Get information about upcoming events, conferences, and webinars in the AIIM field.
  • Join Our Community: Connect with fellow enthusiasts, share your own insights, and collaborate on projects.
  • Student Dropout Prediction (SDP) Hub: Explore a dedicated section for our SDP research, offering in-depth insights and resources.
  • Contact Us: Reach out to us easily for inquiries, collaborations, or to join our group.

Our new website is designed to be user-friendly, informative, and interactive. We hope it becomes a valuable resource for all our members and anyone interested in AIIM research.

Visit our website at and take a tour of all its features. We encourage you to explore, provide feedback, and get involved in the discussions.

We would like to extend our gratitude to the team that worked tirelessly to make this website a reality. Your hard work is greatly appreciated.

Thank you for being a part of our AIIM Research Group, and we look forward to sharing and collaborating on this exciting platform. Let’s continue to advance the frontiers of AI, Data Mining, and Machine Learning together!

Best regards, Prof. G. Stilo on behalf of AIIM Research Group
