Serving at KDD 2020 by chairing the Autoencoders Session

August 08, 2020

KDD is the International Conference yearly dedicated to the innovative research on all aspects of knowledge discovery and data mining, ranging from theoretical foundations to novel models and algorithms for data mining problems in science, business, medicine, and engineering. Prof. G. Stilo is honored to serve the KDD community by chairing the Autoencoders Session. Giovanni Stilo will be on stage with papers authors on the 25th of August at 13:30 Pacific Time.
