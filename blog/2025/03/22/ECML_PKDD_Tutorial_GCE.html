Tutorial on Graph Counterfactual Explainability accepted to be held at ECMl-PKDD 2025

March 22, 2025

We are delighted to announce that our proposals for a tutorial has been accepted for presentation at the European Conference on Machine Learning and Principles and Practice of Knowledge Discovery in Databases (ECML-PKDD 2025), scheduled to take place in Porto, Portugal from 15 to 19 September 2025.

As is widely recognized, the tutorial program constitutes a pivotal component of the comprehensive conference agenda. In light of this, we are committed to exerting our utmost efforts to ensure the successful execution of these events. More info at tutorial call.

We cordially invite you not to miss our tutorial session titled

  • “Unraveling Graph Counterfactual Explainability: from Theoretical Foundations to Technical Mastery” Website of the event
